Here are some sample problems from Rosen.

Decimal to Binary Conversion

  1. Convert the decimal expansion of each of these integers to a binary expansion.
    • a) 231
    • b) 4532
    • c) 97644
  2. Convert the decimal expansion of each of these integers to a binary expansion.
    • a) 321
    • b) 1023
    • c) 100632

Binary to Decimal Conversion

  1. Convert the binary expansion of each of these integers to a decimal expansion.
    • a) (1 1111)₂
    • b) (10 0000 0001)₂
    • c) (1 0101 0101)₂
    • d) (110 1001 0001 0000)₂
  2. Convert the binary expansion of each of these integers to a decimal expansion.
    • a) (1 1011)₂
    • b) (10 1011 0101)₂
    • c) (11 1011 1110)₂
    • d) (111 1100 0001 1111)₂

Octal to Binary Conversion

  1. Convert the octal expansion of each of these integers to a binary expansion.
    • a) (572)₈
    • b) (1604)₈
    • c) (423)₈
    • d) (2417)₈

Binary to Octal Conversion

  1. Convert the binary expansion of each of these integers to an octal expansion.
    • a) (1111 0111)₂
    • b) (1010 1010 1010)₂
    • c) (111 0111 0111 0111)₂
    • d) (101 0101 0101 0101)₂

Hexadecimal to Binary Conversion

  1. Convert the hexadecimal expansion of each of these integers to a binary expansion.
    • a) (80E)₁₆
    • b) (135AB)₁₆
    • c) (ABBA)₁₆
    • d) (DEFACED)₁₆
  2. Convert (BADFACED)₁₆ from its hexadecimal expansion to its binary expansion.
  3. Convert (ABCDEF)₁₆ from its hexadecimal expansion to its binary expansion.

Binary to Hexadecimal Conversion

  1. Convert each of the integers in Exercise 6 from a binary expansion to a hexadecimal expansion.
  2. Convert (1011 0111 1011)₂ from its binary expansion to its hexadecimal expansion.
  3. Convert (1 1000 0110 0011)₂ from its binary expansion to its hexadecimal expansion.
